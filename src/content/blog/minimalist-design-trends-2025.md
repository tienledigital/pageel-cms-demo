# Minimalist Design Trends 2025

Minimalism continues to dominate the digital design world. In 2025, we are seeing a focus on "Calm Tech" and high-quality photography.

![Nature Calm](/images/nature.png)

## Key Elements

- **Whitespaces**: Giving content room to breathe.
- **Vibrant Gradients**: used sparingly for impact.
- **Typography as Hero**: Large, clean fonts that tell the story.
