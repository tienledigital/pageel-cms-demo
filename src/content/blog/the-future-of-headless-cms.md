# The Future of Headless CMS

The shift towards Headless CMS has been significant in recent years. By separating the content from the presentation layer, developers gain immense flexibility.

![Tech Visual](/images/tech.png)

## Why Git-based?

Git-based CMS like Pageel offer a unique advantage: they leverage the existing version control system that developers already use. This means:

- No database needed.
- Version history out of the box.
- Seamless developer workflow.
