# Welcome to Pageel CMS

Pageel is a powerful, client-side, Git-based CMS designed specifically for the Astro ecosystem.

## Why Pageel?

1. **No Database**: Your content is stored directly in your Git repository.
2. **Open Source**: 100% free and open source.
3. **Speed**: Lightweight and fast, no bulky backend servers.

Enjoy managing your content with ease!
